Our Work

Raising awareness on clean water use

As obvious as it is, all humans deserve to drink clean water however, due to environmental or natural circumstances it poses a threat to the community.

MAA community aims at educating on the importance of fresh water to those uneducated.

Raising HIV/AIDS awareness among widows

HIV/AIDS is as common as it was ten years ago however, over the years it has become more widepread and many people are more knowledgable.

Reducing stigma and discrimination of people infected with HIV can make improvements in the lives of those infected / sick.

Promote economic empowerment among the widows

As women who have been widowed, it can pose as a struggle to find and attain financial stability and hence, this NGO aims at ensuring women have job to guarantee them a secure future.

sets a direct path towards gender equality, poverty eradication and inclusive economic growth.
