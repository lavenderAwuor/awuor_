MAA WIDOWS AND OVC

MAA Widows, Orphans, and Vulnerable Children is a community-based organization (CBO. It was formally registered in 2022. Currently, our organization comprises 300 widows distributed across 10 wards within the county, with approximately 30 widows in each ward.
