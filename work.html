Our Work

Our Focus Areas

Raising awareness on clean water use

As obvious as it is, all humans deserve to drink clean water however, due to environmental or natural circumstances it poses a threat to the community.

MAA community aims at educating on the importance of fresh water to those uneducated.

Raising HIV/AIDS awareness among widows

HIV/AIDS is as common as it was ten years ago however, over the years it has become more widepread and many people are more knowledgable.

Reducing stigma and discrimination of people infected with HIV can make improvements in the lives of those infected / sick.

Promote economic empowerment among the widows

As women who have been widowed, it can pose as a struggle to find and attain financial stability and hence, this NGO aims at ensuring women have job to guarantee them a secure future.

sets a direct path towards gender equality, poverty eradication and inclusive economic growth.

Offer psychosocial support to Widows

It is our mission to help the widows in our organization with all the psychological help they require at their own individual levels.

By doing this, we are able to not only provide emotional support for the women, but allow them a smooth transition to society. This support looks like offering weekly counselling sessions to the widows on grief and counselling to assist them in the five stages of bereavement.

Spread Awareness on Female Gential Mutilation(FGM)

It is shocking to say that in today's society there are remote communities in the rural areas of Kenya that practice FGM hence MAA widows and OVC actively works on educating all audiences on the vast effects of this cultural practices. By doing this, multitudes of women are able to live healthier and fulfilling lives.

Spread awareness on Gender Based Violence(GBV)

As MAA Widows and Orphaned and Vulnerable Children(OVC), we prioritise the welfare of all women especially those who fall victim to the hands of men. We aim to educate all and every one on the dangers of domestic violence as well as preventative measures.
